```python
# Count from zero to nine by one.
for i in range(10):
    print(i)
print()

# Count from zero to eight by two.
for i in range(0, 10, 2):
    print(i)
print()

# Count from 100 down to 70 by three.
for i in range(100, 69, -3):
    print(i)
```
